About Me

Christine Sun is a sophomore at Georgetown University. With a major in Science, Technology, and International Affairs, Christine seeks to become the bridge between technology and society. She’s especially interested in the positive and negative implications of emerging technologies.

Having spent half of her life in China and the other half in the U.S, Christine’s also passionate about U.S-China relations. She approaches this topic from a gender angle. Currently, Christine is working on an independent project that will remember the Chinese #MeToo movement in words.

Christine believes in the concept of working for what you love. Before she finds her direction, however, she’s currently exploring opportunities in product management and consulting. She summarizes her reflection about those directions on this website for herself and hopefully, for others as well.
